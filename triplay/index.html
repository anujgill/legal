Privacy Policy

TriPlay — by A2H Software

Effective: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how A2H Software (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use TriPlay (the “App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is written in plain language so you can understand exactly what we do — and do not — do with your information.

1. Information We Collect

TriPlay does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other identifying information.

The only data the App stores is your game progress (current level, coins earned, items unlocked, and settings such as sound and haptic preferences). This data is stored exclusively on your local device using the device’s built-in storage (SQLite). It never leaves your device.

✅  No personal data is ever collected, stored on our servers, or shared with third parties.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

To be explicit, we do not collect or access any of the following:

• Personal identifiers (name, email, phone, address)
• Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, serial number)
• Location data (GPS, IP address, or approximate location)
• Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
• Camera, microphone, contacts, or any device sensors
• Browsing history or data from other apps

3. Local Storage

Your game progress is saved automatically on your device so that your level, coins, and unlocked items persist between sessions. This data is stored in an on-device SQLite database and is never transmitted anywhere.

You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app’s storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App. Clearing app data will reset your game progress.

4. Third-Party Services

TriPlay is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy as part of operating the Play Store. We encourage you to review Google’s Privacy Policy.

The App itself does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or social media platforms.

5. Children’s Privacy

TriPlay is designed to be suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, there is no risk to children’s privacy. We comply with applicable children’s privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play’s Families Policy.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit any personal data, the risk of a data breach involving your personal information is zero. Your game data remains on your device, protected by your device’s own security mechanisms.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable laws. When we make changes, we will update the “Effective Date” at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after a revised Privacy Policy is posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete on our end. All game data resides on your own device, and you have full control over it through your device’s app settings.

If you have any questions about your privacy or believe something in this policy is inaccurate, please contact us and we will respond promptly.
